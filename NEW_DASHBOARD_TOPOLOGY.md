## 1. The Problem: A Flat & Inefficient Structure

After analyzing the current system and your original planning documents, it's clear that the current 11-tab dashboard, while functional, is not intuitive. It presents a flat list of features rather than guiding the user through a logical workflow.

| Current Problem | Description |
|---|---|
| **Cognitive Overload** | 11 separate tabs are too many to process, making it hard to know where to start. |
| **Disconnected Workflow** | Related functions like `Discovery` and `Offers`, or `Assets` and `Landing Pages`, are separated, forcing users to jump between tabs to complete a single task. |
| **Unclear Purpose** | The `Missions` tab, a critical AI orchestration layer, is not clearly defined and its importance is lost among other tabs. |
| **Lack of Hierarchy** | There is no distinction between high-level strategic functions (like `Discovery`) and low-level system settings (like `Integrations`). |
